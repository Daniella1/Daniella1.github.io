## Journal Papers

**Understanding URDF: A Dataset and Analysis**  
*Daniella Tola* and Peter Corke
*IEEE Robotics and Automation Letters*, Vol. 9, No. 5, pp. 4479-486, 2024  
[[PDF arXiv]](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2308.00514) [[DOI]](https://doi.org/10.1109/LRA.2024.3381482) [[Code]](https://github.com/Daniella1/urdf_files_dataset) [[BibTeX]](assets/citations/ieee_urdf_dataset_bibtex.bib)

Systematic comparison revealed significant discrepancies between URDF files and between different parsers, highlighting inconsistencies in the de-facto robot modeling standard.

## Conference Papers

**Understanding URDF: A Survey Based on User Experience**  
*Daniella Tola* and Peter Corke
*IEEE 19th International Conference on Automation Science and Engineering (CASE)*, Auckland, New Zealand, 2023  
[[PDF arXiv]](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2302.13442) [[DOI]](https://doi.org/10.1109/CASE56687.2023.10260660) [[Code]](https://github.com/Daniella1/urdf_survey_material) [[BibTeX]](assets/citations/ieee_urdf_survey_bibtex.bib)

> Conducted a user survey with 500+ participants on the use of URDF, its advantages, perceived challenges, and future prospectives. 
We find that the majority believe URDF will be used in the future, while many were uncertain whether a new format would take over.

## Theses

**Enabling Digitalization in Modular Robotic Systems Integration**  
*Daniella Tola*  
Ph.D. Dissertation, Aarhus University, 2023  &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
[ðŸ“„ Thesis PDF arXiv](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2401.02227)

Research on digitalisation of modular robotic systems integration, addressing key challenges including knowledge silos, vendor lock-in, and inefficient integration processes.
Contributions include: developing a configurator for robotic systems inspired by PC configuration, comprehensive analysis of the URDF standard (500+ survey participants, 33k+ dataset views), literature review on Digital Twins in manufacturing, and outline of how these approaches reduce integration time and costs.

*Daniella Tola*

Research on developing safety cases for complex systems operating in dynamic environments.
